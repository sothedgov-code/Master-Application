Eligibility Criteria:
• FATA domicile.
• Secured at least 60% marks or minimum CGPA 2.5 in graduation level exams held 2023-24, 2024-25, 2025-26.
• Admission as full-time student in current year in partner universities in Pakistan.
• Declared monthly income of parents ≤ Rs. 60,000.
• Children of Govt. employees BPS 1-4 or civilians martyred exempted from income condition.
• Not availing any other educational scholarship this year.
Note:
• Students who have already passed/possessed MA/MSc/MS/MPhil degrees are not eligible.
